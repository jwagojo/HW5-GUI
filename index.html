How to Play

Drag word tiles to the board to form words. First tile must be at the center square.

  • Validate Word - Confirms the validity of the word and updates score.
  • Recall Tile - Undo last tile placement.
  • Refresh Tiles - Discard rack and draw new tiles.
  • Garbage Bin - Drag tiles here to discard them.
